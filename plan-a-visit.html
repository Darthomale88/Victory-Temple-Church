Before You Arrive

What Happens When I Arrive?

01

Park & Enter

Pull into our main lot off Acts Dr. Greeters in blue vests will point you to the nearest entrance.

02

Get Welcomed

Our welcome team will greet you at the door and help you find a seat or answer any questions.

03

Drop Off Kids

Check in children at the Kids Ministry desk just inside the main entrance — it only takes a minute.

04

Enjoy the Service

Services run about 90 minutes and include worship, prayer, and a message from Pastor Williamson.

Welcoming Guests
Come As You Are

What Should I Wear?

Come as you are. Some people dress casually, and others dress more formally. You are welcome either way — we care about you being here, not what you're wearing.

We offer nursery care, a dedicated children's ministry, and a youth class during every service — each staffed by background-checked volunteers who love kids well.
